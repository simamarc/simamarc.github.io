About

The Philosophy:

Currently on a mission: searching for a greatest balance in my life. One part of this project: to love what you do for a living. I do (most of the time). I feed off of high energy collaboration and am thirsty for knowledge.

Graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design in 2011. All through my college years and after, I've been working on logos, layouts, prints and pretty much everything that involves creating a visual representation of a certain idea. Nowadays, I am also into infographics and illustration. Without aesthetics - there is also functionality I am looking for in a design.

Currently living and working in Vilnius. Though I do like to travel. And to plan. And to drink strong coffee while planning. And to recharge my batteries in the nature.

The Process:

I treat every client the same, with a down-to-earth attitude and an enthusiasm for what I do. I begin a project by understanding my client’s requirements. The brand’s personality, tone of voice, target audience and competitors are all evaluated. After a personalized planning and strategy - creativity happens. ~***~
