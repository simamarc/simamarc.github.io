About

The Philosophy:

Currently on a mission to find exciting and creative graphic design projects where both right and left parts of the brain would be stimulated.

Graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design in 2011. All through my college years and after, I've been working on logos, layouts, print/digital design and pretty much everything that involves creating a visual representation of a certain idea. I am also into illustration, both: digital and traditional. Without aesthetics - there is also functionality I am looking for in a design. I feed off of high energy collaboration and am thirsty for knowledge.

Currently creating in London. Though I do like to travel. And to make plans. And to drink the darkest coffee while planning. And to recharge my batteries in the nature.

The Process:

I treat every client the same, with a down-to-earth attitude and an enthusiasm for what I do. I begin a project by understanding my client’s requirements. The brand’s personality, tone of voice, target audience and competitors are all evaluated. After a personalized planning and strategy - creativity happens. ~***~
